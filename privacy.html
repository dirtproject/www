Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:
• name
• contact information

We collect your personal information in order to:
• make a Restaurant reservation

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter your name, phone number and email address, we will be unable to make a table reservation for you. We keep your information safe by storing it on a secure booking database and only Haukai Restaurant staff can access it.

We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by erasing the digital records.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
